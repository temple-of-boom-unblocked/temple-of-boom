Temple of Boom - Play Free Online

Trapped in a monster-infested temple with nothing but your weapon and your reflexes. Temple of Boom is a fast-paced pixel-art platformer where you fight through waves of skeleton warriors, deadly traps, and bizarre beasts to escape alive. Go solo or grab a friend for local two-player action.

Game Modes

  • Campaign Mode — Battle through three temples filled with enemies and traps. Each temple gets harder, demanding sharper reflexes and smarter strategy to survive.
  • Endless Mode — Face wave after wave of increasingly powerful enemies. No checkpoints, no mercy — just survive as long as you can.

Weapons & Power-Ups

Unlock an arsenal of pistols, rifles, enchanted bows, and more from weapon crates scattered across levels. Each weapon plays differently — experiment to find your edge against tougher enemies.

Controls

Single Player:

  • ← / → — Move
  • ↑ — Jump / Double Jump
  • ↓ — Pick Up Weapon
  • Z — Shoot
  • X — Switch Weapon

Two Player:

  • Player 1: A/D move, W jump, S pick up, C shoot, V switch
  • Player 2: Arrows move, ↑ jump, ↓ pick up, K shoot, L switch
